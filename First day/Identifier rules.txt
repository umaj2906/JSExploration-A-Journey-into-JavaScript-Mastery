All JavaScript variables must be identified with unique names (identifiers).

Names can contain letters, digits, underscores and dollar signs.(no space).
bame must begins with letters
names can also begins with $ and _.
for example:
$hlo and _num are applicable @hlo ans 1num is not applicable

for example:- let name & let Name are different variables
